A Brief Research Outlook

Steepest Entropy Ascent

The theory of steepest entropy ascent (SEA) is an interesting approach to understand decoherence in quantum systems. The foundational concept is to introduce the second law of thermodynamics at the level of fundamental theory. Therefore, we can get a theory that maximizes the local entropy production during the evolution of a quantum system. This evolution along the path of "steepest" entropy production is determinded by the generators of motion that produce conserved quantities, such as, energy, probability, number of particles and so on. Developed mainly by Prof. Gian Paolo Beretta, a repository of his work can be explored here.
